Cragmaps

  Cragmaps is a website dedicated to modernizing climbing area information. It was inspired by numerous hours spent lost at the crag, as well as by the lack of good online tools to aid in location. I hope that it will reduce time wandering around and increase time spent climbing.

  Cragmaps was created using Google Earth, Leaflet, and Tilemill. The polygons, points, and lines describing areas, boulders, and rock climbs were digitized using the help of locals, aerial photography, and guidebooks. While I tried my best to make everything as accurate as possible, this map was created by a lonely dirtbag and it is more than likely that some/all of the data is shoddy. If you find an error, send me an email and I'll do my best to fix it.
